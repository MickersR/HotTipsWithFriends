Easy Tip Submission

Select your winning teams for each AFL game and enter your margin prediction for the final game of the round.

Secure Encryption

Your tips are encrypted with a password of your choice, creating a secure string that can be shared safely.

Easy Sharing

Share your encrypted tip string with friends. They can decode it using the password to view your predictions.

How It Works

Submit Your Tips
  1. Enter your name
  2. Select winning teams for each game
  3. Enter margin prediction for the final game
  4. Set a password for encryption
  5. Get your encrypted tip string
View Someone's Tips
  1. Get the encrypted string from a friend
  2. Get the password from them
  3. Enter both in the "View Tips" section
  4. Click decrypt to view their predictions
  5. See their name, tips, and margin
